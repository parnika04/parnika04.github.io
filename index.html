Classic Apple Pie

Prep time: 20 minutes | Cook time: 45 minutes

Indulge in the irresistible harmony of sweet, autumn apples and warm, enchanting cinnamon with this classic apple pie recipe. Every bite offers a delightful fusion of flavors that’s perfect for cosy gatherings and cherished family dinners. Treat yourself and your loved ones to a slice of comfort and joy with this timeless favorite, sure to bring smiles and satisfaction to any occasion.

Ingredients:

4 cups of sliced apples
1 cup of sugar
1 tablespoon of cinnamon
1/2 cup of butter
1 tablespoon of lemon juice
1 pie crust

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and salt. Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add ice water, tossing with a fork until dough comes together.
3. Divide the dough in half. Roll out one half and fit it into a 9-inch pie dish. Set aside.
4. In another bowl, combine apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon juice, and flour. Pour the apple mixture into the pie crust.
5. Roll out the remaining dough and place it over the filling. Trim, seal, and flute the edges. Cut slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
6. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly. Allow to cool before serving.
